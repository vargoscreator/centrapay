1. About This Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how CetraPay S.p.A. (“CetraPay”, “we”, “us” or “our”) uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website https://cetrapay.pro (the “Website”). It describes what cookies are, what types we use, why we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

To understand how we process and protect your personal data, please also refer to our Privacy Policy.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They store information about your browsing session, device settings, and preferences. Cookies do not typically contain personal data, but they may be linked to personal data we store or process about you.

Cookies help improve the functionality, security, and performance of our Website and enable a more personalized user experience.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following categories of cookies:
Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential to enable core functionality of the Website, such as secure login, session management, and fraud prevention. Without them, the Website cannot function properly.

  • Example: session ID, login token, security tokens
Performance & Analytics Cookies

These cookies collect anonymized data about how visitors use the Website, such as page views and error reports, to help us improve its functionality.

  • Example: Google Analytics (if used), visitor frequency
Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences and choices, such as language settings or previously entered data, to improve your experience.

  • Example: saved preferences, region settings
Marketing & Targeting Cookies

These cookies track browsing behavior to help deliver personalized content or advertisements and measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.

  • Example: Google Ads, Meta Pixel, LinkedIn Insights (if applicable)

Note: Any third-party cookie usage is governed by the respective provider’s privacy policy.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage or disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. You may also use our website’s cookie banner or consent manager (if implemented) to adjust your preferences for non-essential cookies.

Please note:

  • Disabling strictly necessary cookies may limit your access to certain features.
  • Disabling cookies does not delete previously set cookies — you must do this manually via your browser.

Instructions for managing cookies in popular browsers:

  • Chrome: chrome://settings/cookies
  • Firefox: about:preferences#privacy
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy
  • Edge: edge://settings/content/cookies

5. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, regulation, or our business practices. Any updates will be posted on this page with the revised “Effective Date.”

We recommend reviewing this Policy regularly to stay informed.
